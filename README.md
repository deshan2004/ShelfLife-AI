# ShelfLife AI 🛒📉

**Eliminating Expiry Losses, Maximizing Retail Profits**

ShelfLife AI is a digital assistant built to monitor retail inventory 24/7. It is specifically designed for small-scale retail environments to prevent financial losses before they occur.

---

## 🚀 The Problem
* **Revenue Leakage:** Small shops lose 5-15% of annual profits due to unnoticed expired items.
* **Manual Tracking Failure:** Managing 1,000+ items with a "pen and paper" log is impossible.
* **Reputation Risk:** Selling expired products leads to loss of trust and potential legal liabilities.

## ✨ Key Features
* **AI-OCR Scanning:** Uses Optical Character Recognition to read expiry dates directly from packaging—ideal for local products without barcodes.
* **Adaptive Notification Engine:** Tiered alerts for items expiring in 30 days, 7 days, or 48 hours.
* **Smart Flash Sale Triggers:** Suggests discount strategies like "Buy 1 Get 1" or "30% Off" for items expiring within 72 hours.
* **Offline-First (PWA):** Works offline for shops with unstable internet and syncs data once the connection returns.
* **AI Recipe Suggestions:** Suggests recipes using near-expiry ingredients to minimize waste.

## 🏗 Technical Architecture
* **Frontend:** React.js
* **Backend:** Node.js 
* **Database:** Firebase 
* **AI Layer:** Tesseract.js / Google Vision API

## 📊 Business Impact
* **Cost Recovery:** Recovers lost revenue by digitizing traditional inventory.
* **Efficiency:** Reduces time spent on manual stock-checking by 90%.
