# ShelfLife AI 🛒📉

[cite_start]**Eliminating Expiry Losses, Maximizing Retail Profits** [cite: 4]

[cite_start]ShelfLife AI is a digital assistant built to monitor retail inventory 24/7. [cite: 38] [cite_start]It is specifically designed for small-scale retail environments to prevent financial losses before they occur. [cite: 39, 41]

## 🚀 The Problem
* [cite_start]**Revenue Leakage:** Small shops lose 5-15% of annual profits due to unnoticed expired items. [cite: 17]
* [cite_start]**Manual Tracking Failure:** Managing 1,000+ items with a "pen and paper" log is impossible. [cite: 18]
* [cite_start]**Reputation Risk:** Selling expired products leads to loss of trust and potential legal liabilities. [cite: 20, 22]

## ✨ Key Features
* [cite_start]**AI-OCR Scanning:** Uses Optical Character Recognition to read expiry dates directly from packaging—ideal for local products without barcodes. [cite: 80]
* [cite_start]**Adaptive Notification Engine:** Tiered alerts for items expiring in 30 days, 7 days, or 48 hours. [cite: 50]
* [cite_start]**Smart Flash Sale Triggers:** Suggests discount strategies like "Buy 1 Get 1" or "30% Off" for items expiring within 72 hours. [cite: 57]
* [cite_start]**Offline-First (PWA):** Works offline for shops with unstable internet and syncs data once the connection returns. [cite: 81]
* [cite_start]**AI Recipe Suggestions:** Suggests recipes using near-expiry ingredients to minimize waste. [cite: 40, 130]
